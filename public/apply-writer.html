Apply to be a Writer

We’re looking for articulate, passionate voices who can spark thoughtful debate. As a writer for Open to Debate you’ll have the opportunity to:

  • Create engaging debates and articles.
  • Reach a wide audience of thoughtful readers.
  • Gain exposure and build your portfolio.
  • Enjoy exclusive writer tools and editorial support.

If you believe you have what it takes, please send an email to apply@opentodebate.com with a brief introduction, your writing samples, and why you’d be a great fit.

We look forward to hearing from you and potentially welcoming you to our vibrant community of debaters.
